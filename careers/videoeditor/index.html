Video Editor

📍 Remote 💼 Freelance / Part-Time 📅 Immediate 🎓 Graduate enrolled/completed

🔧 Requirements

✅ Must-Have:

  • Proficiency in Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects
  • Minimum 2+ years of video editing experience
  • Strong understanding of the type of content we create
  • Must have previous experience working with a creator
  • Excellent English communication skills
  • Strong time management and punctuality
  • Ability to collaborate and take feedback constructively
  • Must be currently enrolled in or have completed a graduate-level program

💡 Nice-to-Have (Plus):

  • Engineering background
  • Prior experience with competitive exams like GATE, UPSC, or IIT JEE

✨ Expectations

  • Creatively identify portions of videos worth turning into YouTube Shorts and clips.
  • Ensure each short is 55 - 179 seconds long.
  • Clips are 8 to 12 minutes long.
  • Add accurate subtitles (English).
  • Translation needed for Hindi Content.
  • Match the style and energy of our existing content.
